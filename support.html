Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fest Finder ?

Fest Finder is an organization that advertises 4 different festivals around the world and helps someone determine which festival they want to visit, in partnership with the festival itself.

How do I buy tickets to one of the desired festivals?

Buying tickets is easy for the desired festival. We have partnered up with Festicket to make buying tickets effortless. Follow the link to their page on any one of the festivals we have to offer and that's it!

What are the festivals Fest Finder has to offer ?

At Fest Finder, we offer 4 world-famous music festivals to choose from:

  • Tomorrowland Muisc Festival, Belgium.. #1 In the World.
  • Creamfields Music Festival, England.. #13 In the World.
  • Coachella Music and Arts Festival, California.. #9 In the World.
  • Hideout Music Festival, Croatia.. #21 In the World.

Can I visit the Fest Finder Offices ?

Yes ! You can drop in to our offices at anytime. Our address is Fest Finder, Dingle, Kerry, Ireland
